About J&S Creekside Cabins — Cabin Rentals in Livingston Manor, NY

J&S Creekside Cabins is a family-owned cabin rental property nestled along the Willowemoc Creek in Livingston Manor, NY. Located at 12 Wegman Road in the heart of Sullivan County, the property has welcomed anglers, families, and outdoor enthusiasts to the Catskill Mountains for years. The cabins sit directly on the creek, providing the kind of streamside access that defines a true Catskills getaway and sets this property apart from other options in the region.

The team behind J&S Creekside Cabins understands that cabin rentals Catskills visitors seek more than a roof over their heads. Every cabin on the property is maintained with care, equipped with full kitchens, comfortable sleeping arrangements, and private outdoor spaces that face the water. Guests arrive to find properties that are clean, functional, and designed for the self-sufficient style of travel that brings people to the mountains. This attention to practical detail reflects the values of a family that lives and works in Livingston Manor year-round.

Community ties run deep at J&S Creekside Cabins. The owners are active participants in the Livingston Manor community, supporting local businesses and contributing to the preservation of the Willowemoc watershed that sustains the area's fishing heritage. Guests benefit from this local knowledge through recommendations for dining, trails, and fishing spots that only residents of the 12758 ZIP code can provide. The result is a cabin stay that feels personally guided rather than commercially transactional, a distinction that returning visitors consistently recognize and value.
